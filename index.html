Reflection

Life at GWU so far has taught me many lessons and I feel that I have matured in my time here. Being away from my parents and the rigid high school structure, I'm finding I have much more time that I get to decide what to do with. This has forced me to improve my time management skills and figure out what works best for me to get my work done. I've not only learned to budget time for work but also time for mental and physical health.
